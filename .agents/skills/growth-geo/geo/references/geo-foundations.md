# GEO Foundations

GEO, or generative engine optimization, focuses on helping content surface in AI-generated answers and citations.

## GEO Versus SEO

- SEO optimizes for ranking and click-through in traditional search
- GEO optimizes for citation, extractability, and trust in answer engines

Strong SEO and strong GEO often reinforce each other, but GEO rewards content that is especially easy for retrieval systems to quote and synthesize.

## Where GEO Shows Up

Common answer-engine surfaces include:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Perplexity
- Gemini

Each engine differs in citation style, but they all benefit from clear entities, explicit facts, and structured content.

## Retrieval Factors To Optimize

Focus on:

- semantic relevance to the question
- clear keyword and entity alignment
- authority and credential signals
- freshness where the topic changes quickly
- source diversity and specificity

## Content That Tends To Be Cited

- original data or statistics
- attributed expert quotes
- clear definitions
- step-by-step instructions
- comparison tables
- concise FAQ sections

## GEO Content Checklist

- [ ] page answers a recognizable question clearly
- [ ] important claims are stated plainly and early
- [ ] authorship or expertise is visible
- [ ] facts are attributable and current
- [ ] tables, FAQs, and definitions make extraction easy
- [ ] update timing is visible when freshness matters
